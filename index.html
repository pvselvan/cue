A simple way to think before you say

Microaggression (n) - a subtle but offensive comment or action directed at a minority or other nondominant group that is often unintentional or unconsciously reinforces a stereotype


Cyberbullying is a huge problem in the US and more than 1 in 3 people with internet access have experienced some form of cyberbullying. We can do better. Research shows that when adolescents have the opportunity to rethink a message, 93% of the time, it leads to them rewording their message.


Cue is a google chrome extension that highlights potentially offensive phrases before they are said. We think that most people are unaware that certain words can come across as hurtful. We want to create a more empathetic world, one cue at a time.
